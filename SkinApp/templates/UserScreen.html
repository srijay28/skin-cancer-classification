How to Use the System

  1. Upload a Skin Image: Click on the "Upload Skin Image" button to choose and upload a photo of your skin lesion. Make sure the image is clear and well-lit for accurate analysis.
  2. Review Results: After uploading, our system will process the image and provide you with a detailed analysis. You can view the results and understand the classification through the information displayed.
  3. Logout: When you’re done, click on the "Logout" button to securely end your session. This ensures that your data and results remain confidential.
